Legally Blonde


Legally Blonde is one of the best chick flicks ever made in my opinion. The main character, Elle Woods, is misunderstood and is percieved as a "dumb blonde" by others.
She proves them wrong by going to Harvard Law School and succeeding. Elle never loses her kindness even when people treated her terribly. She is an example that you
can be strong and intelligent without losing touch of your femininity.
